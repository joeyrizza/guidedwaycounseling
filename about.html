About Casie Rizza, LCPC-C, MA

Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor

Welcome

I'm Casie Rizza, and I'm honored that you're considering therapy as a step toward healing and growth. I believe that each person's story is unique and deserves to be heard with empathy, authenticity, and unconditional acceptance.

My approach to therapy is rooted in the belief that you are the expert on your own life. My role is to walk alongside you, providing support, guidance, and evidence-based tools as you navigate your challenges and discover your path forward.

My Philosophy

I use an integrated bio/psycho/social/spiritual approach to therapy, recognizing that healing involves the whole person. I believe in:

Empathy & Authenticity

Creating a genuine therapeutic relationship where you feel truly seen and understood.

Unconditional Acceptance

Providing a non-judgmental space where all parts of your story are welcome.

Client-Centered Care

Honoring your unique needs, values, and goals throughout our work together.

Trauma-Informed Practice

Understanding how past experiences shape present challenges and working gently with your nervous system.

Holistic Healing

Addressing the interconnected nature of mind, body, emotions, and spirit.

Evidence-Based Methods

Drawing from proven therapeutic approaches while tailoring treatment to your individual needs.

Areas of Focus

I specialize in working with adults facing:

  • Trauma & PTSD: Healing from past wounds with trauma-informed, somatic approaches
  • Anxiety & Depression: Finding relief and developing lasting coping strategies
  • Religious Trauma / Church Hurt: Processing spiritual abuse and finding your authentic path
  • Motherhood Challenges: Support through pregnancy, postpartum, and the complexities of parenting
  • Life Transitions: Navigating major changes with resilience and clarity
  • ADHD: Understanding your unique brain and developing practical strategies
  • Grief & Loss: Processing loss and finding meaning in difficult seasons
  • Highly Sensitive Persons: Embracing sensitivity while building healthy boundaries
  • Relationship Issues & Codependency: Developing healthier patterns in relationships

Therapeutic Approaches

I integrate multiple evidence-based modalities to create a treatment plan tailored to your needs:

Person-Centered Therapy

You are the expert on your life; I provide empathy and unconditional positive regard

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns and behaviors

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

Exploring and healing the different parts of yourself with compassion

Trauma-Focused Therapy

Gentle, effective approaches to processing traumatic experiences

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Cultivating present-moment awareness and self-compassion

Somatic Techniques

Working with the body and nervous system to release stored trauma

Psychodynamic Therapy

Understanding how past experiences shape current patterns

Christian Counseling

Optional integration of faith for those who desire it
