Life can feel overwhelming. Whether you're facing anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, or the unique challenges of motherhood, you don't have to navigate these struggles alone. At Guided Way Counseling, I provide a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your experiences, process your emotions, and discover your path to healing.

My Approach

I use an integrated bio/psycho/social/spiritual approach, drawing from evidence-based modalities including:

  • Person-Centered Therapy
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Internal Family Systems (IFS)
  • Trauma-Focused Therapy
  • Mindfulness-Based Therapy
  • Christian Counseling (optional)

Every person's journey is unique. I meet you where you are with empathy, authenticity, and unconditional acceptance.
