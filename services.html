Services & Specialties

Comprehensive mental health support for adults

I offer individual therapy for adults dealing with a wide range of mental health concerns. Using an integrated, evidence-based approach, we'll work together to address your unique needs and goals.

"What's wounded in relationship must be healed in relationship. You deserve to be seen, heard, known, and accepted exactly as you are."

🧠

Depression & Anxiety

Living with depression or anxiety can feel like carrying a heavy weight. You might experience persistent sadness, worry, difficulty concentrating, changes in sleep or appetite, or feeling overwhelmed by everyday tasks. Remember: struggling doesn't mean you're defective—it means you're human.

Together, we'll explore the root causes of your symptoms and develop practical strategies to help you feel more balanced and at peace. In a space where you're truly seen and heard, you'll learn to challenge unhelpful thought patterns, regulate your emotions, and rediscover joy in your life.

I can help with: Generalized anxiety, social anxiety, panic, major depression, persistent depressive disorder, stress management

💚

Trauma & PTSD

Trauma can leave lasting impacts on your sense of safety, relationships, and daily functioning. Whether you've experienced a single traumatic event or chronic/complex trauma, healing is possible. What's wounded in relationship must be healed in relationship—and therapy provides that healing connection.

I provide trauma-informed care using gentle, effective approaches that honor your pace and help you process difficult experiences without re-traumatizing. In a space where you can be fully known and accepted, we'll work to release stored trauma from your body and nervous system, helping you reclaim a sense of safety and wholeness.

I can help with: PTSD, complex trauma, childhood trauma, acute trauma response, trauma-related anxiety and depression

🕊️

Church Hurt / Religious Trauma / Spiritual Abuse

Religious trauma and spiritual abuse can be deeply painful, often leaving you feeling confused, guilty, or questioning your identity and beliefs. Perhaps you've felt unseen, unheard, or unaccepted in religious spaces. This is a safe place where you can be fully known and accepted without judgment.

Whether you're deconstructing your faith, processing church hurt, or healing from spiritual abuse, I understand the unique challenges of religious trauma. You deserve to explore your authentic beliefs and feelings in a space of unconditional acceptance. I can integrate faith into our work if you desire, or we can focus purely on healing without any religious framework—your comfort and needs guide our approach.

I can help with: Religious trauma, spiritual abuse, faith deconstruction, church hurt, toxic religious environments

🤱

Motherhood & Postpartum Support

The journey through pregnancy, postpartum, and motherhood can be both beautiful and overwhelming. You don't have to suffer in silence or feel guilty for struggling. Struggling doesn't mean you're failing—it means you're human, navigating one of life's most profound transitions.

I provide compassionate support for all stages of motherhood, including prenatal anxiety, postpartum depression and anxiety, birth trauma, grief related to pregnancy loss or infertility, and the challenges of adjusting to motherhood. In a space where you're truly heard and understood, we'll work together to help you feel more confident, connected, and supported.

I can help with: Postpartum depression and anxiety, pregnancy-related concerns, birth trauma, infertility grief, motherhood adjustment, parenting stress

🌿

ADHD

Living with ADHD can mean struggling with focus, organization, time management, emotional regulation, and feeling misunderstood. Understanding your unique brain is the first step toward thriving.

I'll help you understand how ADHD affects your life and develop practical strategies that work with your brain, not against it. Together we'll build systems, address emotional challenges, and help you embrace your strengths while managing difficulties.

I can help with: Adult ADHD, executive function challenges, emotional dysregulation, time management, organization, relationship impacts of ADHD

🌅

Life Transitions

Major life changes—even positive ones—can be disorienting and stressful. Whether you're changing careers, moving, ending a relationship, or facing other significant transitions, support can make all the difference.

I'll help you navigate uncertainty, process complex emotions, and develop resilience as you move through change. Together we'll find clarity about your values and goals, helping you move forward with confidence.

I can help with: Career changes, relationship transitions, relocation, identity shifts, major life decisions, adjustment challenges

💔

Grief & Loss

Grief is a natural response to loss, but it can feel overwhelming and isolating. There's no "right way" to grieve, and healing doesn't mean forgetting.

I provide a compassionate space to process your grief at your own pace. Whether you're grieving the death of a loved one, the loss of a relationship, or other significant losses, we'll work together to help you find meaning, honor your experience, and gradually rebuild your life.

I can help with: Death of a loved one, complicated grief, anticipatory grief, loss of relationship, loss of identity or dreams

✨

Highly Sensitive Persons (HSP)

Being highly sensitive means you process things more deeply, are more affected by your environment, and feel emotions intensely. This is a trait, not a flaw.

I'll help you understand and embrace your sensitivity as a strength while developing healthy boundaries and coping strategies. Together we'll work on managing overstimulation, honoring your needs, and thriving as a sensitive person in a demanding world.

I can help with: Overstimulation, boundary setting, emotional overwhelm, sensory sensitivities, embracing sensitivity

💕

Relationship Issues & Codependency

Healthy relationships require boundaries, communication, and a strong sense of self. If you struggle with codependency, people-pleasing, or recurring relationship patterns, therapy can help.

We'll explore your attachment patterns, develop healthier relationship skills, and help you build the self-worth needed for fulfilling connections. This includes addressing issues like infidelity, communication breakdowns, and establishing boundaries.

I can help with: Codependency, people-pleasing, relationship conflict, infidelity recovery, attachment issues, boundary development
